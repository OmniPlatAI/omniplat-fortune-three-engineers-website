Environment, Health & Safety

Environment Management System

Our teams at Fortune Three are cognizant of our need as a company to protect the environment in which we work. We therefore have a functioning environmental management system that avoids or minimizes damages to the environment.

By conducting regular assessments, trainings, and formulating environmental drills, we continue to enhance our environmental management system.

Safety Management System

In order to ensure safety, we have established and improved our safety management system. Through intensive training and education, the supervision of processes, summary and feedback systems, we have achieved a closed-loop safety management system.

We ensure the effective operations of our safety system, protecting all stakeholders in every project we undertake.
